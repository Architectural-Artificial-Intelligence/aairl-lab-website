Lielle Brenner is an alumna of AAIRL. Her work was connected to the lab's Design AI research, which studies how artificial intelligence can support architectural creativity, design communication, and human-centered evaluation.

Her contribution forms part of the lab's broader effort to understand how computational tools can expand, rather than replace, architectural reasoning and design judgment.
