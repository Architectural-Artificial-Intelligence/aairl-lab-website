Dr. Haya Brama is a researcher and lab manager at AAIRL, specializing in deep learning, machine learning, and AI applications for architectural and urban systems.

She completed her Ph.D. in neuroscience at Bar-Ilan University, where she investigated biological neural networks through population-dynamics simulations and in-vivo electrophysiological recordings. During her postdoctoral work at Ariel University in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Management, she studied artificial neural networks, cyber-security applications, and search heuristics.

From 2000 to 2023, Haya served as Chief Scientific Officer at ARK, a startup focused on AI-based automated architectural design. Her experience connects scientific modeling, applied AI, and the practical development of computational tools for design and planning.
