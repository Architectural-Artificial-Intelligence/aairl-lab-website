Dr. Haya Brama is a researcher and lab manager at AAIRL, specializing in deep learning, machine learning, and AI applications for architectural and urban systems.

She completed her Ph.D. in neuroscience at Bar-Ilan University, where she studied biological neural networks using population-dynamics modeling and in-vivo electrophysiological recordings. Her postdoctoral research at Ariel University's Department of Industrial Engineering and Management extended this computational foundation to artificial neural networks, cybersecurity applications, and metaheuristic search.

From 2000 to 2023, Haya served as Chief Scientific Officer at ARK, a startup developing AI-based tools for automated architectural design. This trajectory — from systems neuroscience to applied machine learning in the built environment — informs her current work connecting rigorous computational modeling with practical planning and design problems.
