Elior Dahan is a B.Sc. student in Industrial Engineering and Management and an honors-track M.Sc. student in Industrial Engineering and Management. His work at AAIRL contributes to the lab's Urban Digital Twin research.

Elior studies how urban data, computational models, and AI-based analysis can support more transparent and evidence-based planning processes. His work is connected to the lab's effort to develop interoperable digital infrastructure for urban simulation, decision support, and model evaluation.
