Bnaya Unger is a B.Sc. student in Industrial Engineering and Management and an honors-track M.Sc. student in Industrial Engineering and Management. His research at AAIRL focuses on urban renewal decision support.

As part of a collaborative project with Green Group, Bnaya studies parcel aggregation for urban renewal. His work applies machine learning and deep learning methods to identify promising parcel combinations and support more transparent, evidence-based planning decisions.
