Gal Guz is an architecture student, UX/UI designer, and alumnus of AAIRL. His work combines architectural design, interface design, and software development for complex research systems.

At AAIRL, Gal contributed to projects on AI-supported architectural evaluation and urban planning decision support. His work included interface design for systems that predict social impacts of urban renewal and research on whether AI tools can approximate human responses to built spaces.

Gal's design practice spans user requirements, operational concept development, prototyping, user testing, design systems, and development handoff. He works primarily with the React ecosystem and 3D web technologies such as Three.js.
