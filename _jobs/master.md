about:
- Gain research skills, strong portfolio, and a realistic view of academic and applied research
- Complete a research project
- Build methods or tools and validate them empirically
- A written thesis with a clear contribution
- Often a submission to a conference/journal

look:
- Strong motivation for research and writing
- Interest in computational, empirical, or design-oriented research
- Ability to work independently while receiving close supervision
- Relevant skills in design, planning, data analysis, programming, or spatial tools
- Availability for sustained thesis work within ongoing lab projects

Email [Dr. Haya Brama](mailto:hayab@ariel.ac.il) with your CV and transcripts, using the subject line “Master’s Thesis Application”.
In the email body, include:

* Your degree program and year
* A short description of your interests (2–3 topics)
* Relevant skills (software/tools/courses) and your weekly availability


More details are available on Ariel University's [MSc in Architecture website](https://www.ariel.ac.il/wp/architecture/m-d/).
