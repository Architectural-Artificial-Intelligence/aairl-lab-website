What We Do

AAIRL focuses on concrete problems in urban design and planning where AI can augment human capabilities. Our core research directions include:
  • Human-AI interaction in design
  • Generative architectural and urban design
  • Built-environment analysis
  • Built-environment simulations
Much of our work is conducted with industry and government partners, so research is tested against real constraints: incomplete data, conflicting objectives, regulations, and implementation realities.
